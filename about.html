About

Hello there!
I'm Ophir, a graphic designer with a degree in Visual Communication from HIT Institution. I've spent two years immersed in product design at Amdocs, getting my hands dirty and diving deep into the world of design.
When it comes to my craft, I'm all about blending creativity with strategy. I thrive on crafting concept-driven designs that not only look amazing but also resonate deeply with users. I'm constantly seeking new ways to push the boundaries of creativity and bring fresh ideas to the table.
One of the things that really lights me up is delving into the world of user experience. I'm fascinated by understanding user behavior, uncovering pain points, and crafting solutions that make the user journey smoother and more enjoyable. 
Ready to take on my next challenge and become part of a large, diverse and cohesive team.
